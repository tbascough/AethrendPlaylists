Set The Mood

Perfect Roleplay Moods for your game!

We've scoured the far realms to find music that will elevate any roleplaying game. Each playlist maintains a consistent energy and tone without being overly distracting. We've carefully avoided recognizable themes, prioritizing the work of smaller independent composers over blockbuster soundtracks and AAA game scores (though we've sprinkled in a few of our favorites for good measure ;) The playlists are sorted by themeatic locale named after similar regions in our own original DnD Wyrld.

We're always on the lookout for more hidden gems from independent artists to enrich our playlists, so please send us your recommendations so we can all support one another!
